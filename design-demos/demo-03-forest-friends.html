The Little Rabbit

Once upon a time, there was a little rabbit named Benny. He lived in a small hole under a big oak tree in the forest. Benny was very curious and loved to explore.

One sunny morning, Benny hopped out of his hole. He saw a bright orange carrot in the garden. "Yum!" he said. "I want that carrot for my breakfast."

Benny hopped quickly across the grass. He looked left and right. Then he pulled the carrot out of the ground. It was the biggest carrot he had ever seen!
